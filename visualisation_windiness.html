Windiness

The above scatter plot comparing wind speed to the latitude clearly shows that there is both high and low wind speed across all degrees of the latitude. An obvious observation is that from a wind speed of 1 m/s to about 5 m/s shows a huge cluster of plots, showing that most of the world experiences wind speed within these numbers on average. If you look to the left of the scatter plot, you will also note that a latitude nearing -60 dgrees have the highest weend speeds, with no other data being recoreded below a wind speed of 11 m/s.
